ABOUT US

Located at Mesquite Metro Airport, Mesquite Aviation offers aircraft rental, aerial photography, tours, discovery flights, and excellent pilot training. Our comprehensive training programs include private, instrument, commercial, CFI, and CFII along with complex endorsements.

We are continuously improving our facilities with major enhancements including an all-new ground testing facility and our own maintenance center, ensuring your training experience is supported by the best infrastructure in the Dallas-Fort Worth area.

Chip Harding

Owner & Aviation Enthusiast

Chip has been an aviation enthusiast his entire life, growing up around pilots and airports. After beginning his flying journey over 30 years ago and undergoing extensive training, Chip acquired Mesquite Aviation in 2011 to pursue his childhood dream of working in the aviation industry. His mission is to bring his love of general aviation to the flight school with continuous improvements. "We remain open to suggestions for improvements and wish to make Mesquite Aviation an asset to Mesquite Metro Airport, the community, and General Aviation."
